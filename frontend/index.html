How it Works

  • Privacy: Your birth year and personal details never leave your device
  • Zero-Knowledge: The proof reveals only that you're over 18, nothing else
  • Unlinkable: Multiple age verifications cannot be linked together
  • Auditable: All issued credentials are recorded in a public Merkle tree
